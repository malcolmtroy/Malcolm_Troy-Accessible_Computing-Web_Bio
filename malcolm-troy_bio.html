Accessibility Interest

My Personal Experience

I am a Type 1 Diabetic and that has greatly impacted my life as I have to deal with issues all of the time revolving around my diet, medical devices, and more. I benefit a great deal from specific designs such as nutrition facts on packiging or menus offering low carb and no sugar options. It is such a blessing to live in this modern age with the advancements we have made and I love learning about how to be more inclusive towards those who deal with disabilities as well.

Interest Beyond

As someone whose major revolves around computers and software, the intersection between accessibility and technology has obviously caught my eye. Features such as high contrast interfaces, text-to-speech, screen reader compatibility, captions, voice navigation, and customizable UI settings show how thoughtful programming can make digital spaces more inclusive. I look forward to researching human-computer interaction in this class and how to improve user experience and incorporate universal design.
